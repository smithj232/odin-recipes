Air Fryer Chicken

Home

A healthy Nutritious Protein

Ingredients

  • Chicken Tenders
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. preheat air fryer 400f for 5 minutes
  2. Pound uncooked tenders until it is evenly thin
  3. pour olive oil in plate and marinate chicken inside
  4. add salt and pepper generously
  5. evenly distrubite chicken in basket and set for 400f 15-20 minutes
