Diet Apple Pie!

A Healthier Alternative To a Classic American Dessert

Ingredients

  • 6 Tart Apples
  • Flour
  • Splenda
  • Salt
  • Cinnamon
  • Butter

Steps

Filling:

  1. Peel Core and Dice Apples
  2. mix together 1tsp of cinnamon a cup of Splenda half a tsp of salt and a quarter cup of flour and half a tsp of nutmeg
  3. Pour over the peeled coored and diced apples and stir

Crust:

  1. Preheat oven to 375f
  2. In a medium size glass mixing bowl pour 2/3 cup of extra virgin olive oil and 1/3 cup cold water stir until emulsified
  3. In a seperate bowl pour 2 cups of flour and 1 tsp of salt stir
  4. pour flour into liquid stir with fork to combine press together into a ball wrap inside cellaphane and let rest in refrigerator for at least 20 minutes
  5. cut refrigerated ball in half put seperate pieces between 2 pieces wax paper and roll until desired shape
  6. brush 9 inch pie dish with olive oil
  7. peel top wax off one dough flip into pie dish so wax is on top and then peel other wax off
  8. pour apple filling into pie dough
  9. repeat step 6 with last dough
  10. brush top of dough with milk and splenda
  11. Poke steam hole in dough/decorative pinch edges the dough's together

Baking Instructions:

  1. bake at 375f 45-55 minutes
